What We Raise?

In a country where agriculture is a vital economic driver, Chicks N' More will make a significant contribution
to the poultry industry by producing high-quality chicken products.

Rhode Island Red

The Rhode Island Red is an American breed of chicken. It is a utility bird, raised for both meat and eggs, and also as a show bird. It is a popular choice for backyard flocks because of its egg laying abilities and hardiness.

Barred Plymouth Rock

Barred Plymouth Rocks are doctile, gentle and super friendly. They are a dual-purpose heritage breed that will produce around 250 brown eggs a year. They are winter hearty and handle the heat well. Their lifespan is 6-8 years, but they can live longer.

Black Australoop

The Black Australorp is a hard working chicken. She is one of the best egg layers around and can lay more than 5 eggs every week. Their egg laying ability and impeccable manners help to make them a wonderful chicken that is well suited to all backyard chicken keepers.
